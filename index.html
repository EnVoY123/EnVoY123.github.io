Chez Marius - kitchen shop in Helsinki

User Research project

Chez Marius is a small brick and mortar shop located in downtown Helsinki. As part of a UX research project, our goal was to study the business and evaluate its services in relation to the customer experience and user satisfaction. For this, we gathered insights on the web services and the retail storefront through observation, interviews and surveys.

Gira La Routa

Participatory design project, University of Trento, Italy

We have decided to undertake this project because it offered a great occasion to involve people working at Giralaruota, they were familiar with this subject, so we could rely on their experience and knowledge. The organization itself expressed the desire to work on this project. The main purpose was to show the nal product at the fair “Fa la cosa giusta”, in order to gain visibility and promote involvement into eco friendly approach of Giralaruota. On our way to succeed we had to deal with short amount of time. During the project, we have implemented eco-charger which allows bike owners charge their bike during the cycling.

Evolo - protect yourself against drone invaders

Product Development project, University of Trento, Italy

Evolo is device which can protect your own house against drone invaders. Connect it to your WIFI router and be safe within 200meters range. Technologies used:
Design: Product-Process Change Matrix, Stakeholders map, Interviews, Focus groups, Concept Development Funnel
IT: Requirements Engineering, Python, WIFI-Shark, HTML/CSS, Arduino/Raspberry, PHP, Parrot Drones

Charge'n'Go - Non-stop Electric vehicle driving

Business design project for Nomadic Power under EIT Summer School

Currently there is a worldwide push on reducing carbon emissions and a big factor of this is converting the fleet of Internal Combustion Engine (ICE) vehicles to Electric Vehicles (EV). Unfortunately, electric vehicles at this point in time have problems that prevent consumers from making the switch. These problems are mainly the limited range due to battery capacity and the long charging times. Our case provider BVB-Innovate developed a hardware solution to solve the problems of range limitations and long charging times.

Jammin - find jammers near to you

Project has been done during the Design of WWW-Services course.

Jammin is a web service developed by using ReactJS + Google Firebase. The service aims to ease the meeting process of like-minded musicians in a specific area. Our focus was on the usability of the app, therefore we developed only limited functionalities (authentication, creation and editing of user profile, search) given the limited time frame available. During this project, I improved my design and project management skills by using different tools such as Personas, Online Surveys, Interviews, Documentations etc.
